At 92NY Talks, the world listens. Bring your next event to New York’s most prestigious venue for civic discourse and the exchange of ideas that shape our world.

92NY is the premier cultural and community center in New York — an intellectual hub where the leading thinkers of our time come to share ideas with an exceptionally engaged audience. But during the Covid-19 pandemic, 92NY’s reach has extended far beyond New York — we have emerged as a global institution. Our talks and other events were viewed by 4.6 million people in over 200 countries last year. We now connect with audiences all over the world.

We regularly host a diverse array of world leaders, film and entertainment artists, Supreme Court Justices, and the sharpest minds in politics and literature. Our guests have included President Bill Clinton, Senator Kristin Gillibrand, Senator Cory Booker, Doris Kearns Goodwin, Maya Angelou, Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg, Robert Caro, Ken Burns, Meryl Streep, Shimon Peres, Justice Stephen Breyer, Stacey Abrams, and many more.
